Premise

Star Wars is an epic space opera franchise created by filmmaker George Lucas. It is set “a long time ago, in a galaxy far, far away”, and its stories involve the struggle between good freedom fighters (either the Rebellion or the Resistance, depending on the time era) and an evil military dictatorship (either the Galactic Empire or the First Order, depending on the time era).

Central to the stories is the Force, a mystical energy field created by all living things throughout the Galaxy. Mastery of the Force can give superhuman powers like telepathy and telekenisis to the good Jedi Knights

Chronology order

Watch episode 4, 5, and 6 before watching episode 1, 2, 3. The main reason is that 4, 5, and 6, which we usually call "the original trilogy", were made before eps. 1-3 came. Another reason is that, eps. 4-6 are much better than eps 1-3. If you start from eps 1, you might finally give up watching another Star Wars movies.

"Rogue One" takes place right before and leading up to the events of "A New Hope" seamlessly. So those are best watched back-to-back. "Solo," meanwhile, takes place after "Revenge of the Sith," but more than a decade before "A New Hope."
